Rocky Mountain National Park is a National Park located in Colorado, USA and takes up 265,461 acres of land. The park is located between Estes Park, CO and Grand Lake, CO. It is known for its mountains, alpine lakes, and variety of wildlife within different environments of the park. The Rocky Mountain National Park Act was signed in 1915, establishing the park and protecting it for the years to come.
